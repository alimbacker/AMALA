NAGORE'S GARDEN RETREAT

Our Story: A Garden of Hospitality

Situated in the spiritual heart of Nagore, Hotel Al-Garden was originally built as a private residence for local nobility. Today, it stands as a testament to the region's rich architectural heritage, seamlessly blending Indo-Islamic design with modern hospitality excellence.

Our garden setting provides a tranquil escape just minutes from the sacred Nagore Dargah. Whether you are a pilgrim, a family, or a business traveller, Hotel Al-Garden welcomes you with warm hospitality and a peaceful atmosphere.
